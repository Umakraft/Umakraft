# Refiner

## Purpose

The **Refiner** department is responsible for transforming trusted information into meaningful and actionable data.

It retrieves verified records from the Vault, performs business logic, calculations, comparisons, and analysis, then produces refined results for the next department within the Refinery.

The Refiner is the analytical engine of UmaKraft.

* Retrieve trusted information from the Vault.
* Calculate daily fan gain.
