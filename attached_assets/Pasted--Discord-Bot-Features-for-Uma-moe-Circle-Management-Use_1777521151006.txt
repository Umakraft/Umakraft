# Fan Tracking Commands

## `/fan_gain`

Displays:

* Total accumulated fan gain from Day 1 up to the current date
* Total fan gain for the last 7 days
* Daily fan gain ranking

Example:

```txt
Koeru
Daily Fan Gain: 3,168,346
Weekly Fan Gain: 18,410,287
Monthly Fan Gain: 56,388,206
Ranking: #1
```

> Do not display total lifetime fans in this command.

---

## `/leaderboard`

Displays rankings from highest to lowest fan gain.

Features:

* Daily fan gain
* Weekly fan gain
* Monthly fan gain
* Adjustable ranking display (Top 10 to Top 30)

Example:

```txt
#1 Koeru
Daily Fan Gain: 3,168,346
Weekly Fan Gain: 18,410,287
Monthly Fan Gain: 56,388,206
```

Additional Features:

* Automatically resets monthly rankings every new month
* Excludes members who already left the club
* Automatically updates every 24 hours
* Marks newly joined members with a `NEW MEMBER` tag

Example:

```txt
Koeru — NEW MEMBER
```

---

## `/total_fan`

Displays only the player's total lifetime fans.

Example:

```txt
Koeru
Rank: #1
Total Fan Count: 258,140,044
```

---

## `/total_circlefan_gain`

Displays the total accumulated fans of the entire circle.

Members who already left the circle must be excluded automatically.

---

## `/circle_master`

Displays the Top 3 daily contributors in calendar format.

Example:

```txt
Day 1 — April 1, 2026
Total Daily Gain: 20,000,000

Top Rank
1. MemberA
Daily Fan Gain: 2,000,000

2. MemberB
Daily Fan Gain: 1,500,000

3. MemberC
Daily Fan Gain: 1,000,000
```

Features:

* Shows records from Day 1 until the current day
* Automatically stops at tally period
* Includes total daily gain of all members

# New Member System

When a new member joins the circle:

## Contribution Rules

* Their first day contribution is counted as `0`
* Fan tracking begins starting the next day

Example:

* If a member joins on April 7:

  * April 7 contribution = 0
  * Tracking starts April 8

This rule applies to:

* `/fan_gain`
* `/leaderboard`

But does NOT apply to:

* `/total_fan`
