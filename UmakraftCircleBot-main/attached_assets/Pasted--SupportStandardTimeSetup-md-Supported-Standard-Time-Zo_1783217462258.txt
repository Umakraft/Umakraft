## Recommended Internal Storage

Always store timestamps in:

- UTC (ISO 8601)
- Example:
  - `2026-07-05T13:45:00Z`

Display to users using their preferred timezone whenever possible.

## Notes

- Prefer region-specific abbreviations (PHT, JST, KST, SGT, etc.) when known.
- Always distinguish Standard Time and Daylight Saving Time where applicable (PST vs PDT, EST vs EDT, etc.).
- UTC should be the authoritative internal reference.
- Display local time only after conversion from UTC.
