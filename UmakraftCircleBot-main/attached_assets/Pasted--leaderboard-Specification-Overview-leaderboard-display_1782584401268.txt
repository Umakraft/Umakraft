# `/leaderboard` Specification

## Overview

`/leaderboard` displays the highest-performing trainers within the Discord server based on accumulated fan gain.

The command is designed to promote healthy competition, recognize top performers, and provide guild leaders with a quick overview of member activity.

All leaderboard responses are generated as Image Reports.

---

# Command

```text
/leaderboard
```

When no autocomplete option is selected, the bot displays the **Daily Leaderboard**.

---

# Autocomplete Options

* Daily
* Weekly
* Monthly

---

# Default Behaviour

```
/leaderboard
```

Automatically displays the **Daily Leaderboard**.

Reason:

Daily rankings are the most frequently viewed and provide immediate feedback to members.

---

# Weekly Leaderboard

Displays:

* Top 10 Weekly Fan Gain

---

# Monthly Leaderboard

Displays:

* Top 10 Monthly Fan Gain
* Monthly Rankings
* Monthly Guild Statistics
* Requesting Member Position

Ranking is calculated using accumulated fan gain during the current month.

# Design Principles

* Every leaderboard is generated as an Image Report.
* Highlight competition rather than simply listing numbers.
* Celebrate top performers with podium styling.
* Always display the requesting member's position.
* Keep the default report concise and mobile-friendly.
* Reserve advanced analytics for future phases.
* Ensure all rankings are calculated from verified stored fan gain data.

---

# Milestone Goal

The `/leaderboard` command should become the primary competitive feature of the bot.

A single image should allow every member to instantly understand:

* Who is leading.
* Their own current position.
* The gap to the next rank.
* Guild completion progress.
* Overall guild performance.

Future milestones will expand the leaderboard into a complete competitive analytics system with historical rankings, movement tracking, achievement badges, advanced statistics, and visual charts while maintaining accurate and reliable data.
